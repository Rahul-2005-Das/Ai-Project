Introduction to Artificial Intelligence

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a rapidly growing field of computer science that focuses on developing systems capable of performing tasks that normally require human intelligence. These tasks include learning from experience, reasoning, problem-solving, understanding natural language, and recognizing patterns. AI enables machines to analyze large amounts of data, make decisions, and improve their performance over time. With advancements in computing power and data availability, AI has become an essential technology, influencing various sectors such as healthcare, education, finance, transportation, and communication. As a result, Artificial Intelligence plays a significant role in shaping modern technology and the future of innovation.
